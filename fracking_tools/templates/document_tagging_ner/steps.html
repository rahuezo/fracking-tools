0. Before You Begin

Before tagging documents you MUST follow the exact instructions provided below.

Instructions

1. Select all the files you want to tag. Your files must be .txt or .docx.

2. Choose a name for the output .zip file.

3. Once you choose which files to upload and enter an output .zip name, you can click on Tag to tag your files using NER.

4. The output zip file will contain all the tagged documents.

Some Background Information

NER can tag documents for persons, organizations, dates, percentages, locations, money, and times.

As an example, look at the following text

In bringing his distinct vision to the Western genre, writer-director Jim Jarmusch has created a quasi-mystical avant-garde drama that remains a deeply spiritual viewing experience. After losing his parents and fiancée, a Cleveland accountant named William Blake (a remarkable Johnny Depp) spends all his money and takes a train to the frontier town of Machine in order to work at a factory.

Applying NER to this text would yield

In bringing his distinct vision to the Western genre, writer-director <PERSON>Jim Jarmusch</PERSON> has created a quasi-mystical avant-garde drama that remains a deeply spiritual viewing experience. After losing his parents and fiancée, a <LOCATION>Cleveland</LOCATION> accountant named <PERSON>William Blake</PERSON> (a remarkable <PERSON>Johnny Depp</PERSON>) spends all his money and takes a train to the frontier town of Machine in order to work at a factory.
